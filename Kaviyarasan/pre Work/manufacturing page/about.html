Our Story

A Legacy of Precision Manufacturing

Founded in 2001 by a team of passionate engineers, FORXA Manufacturing began as a small precision machine shop in Detroit, Michigan. Our founders shared a vision: to create a manufacturing company that would set new standards for quality, innovation, and customer service in the industry.

Over the past 25 years, we've grown into a full-service manufacturing powerhouse with a 200,000 square foot state-of-the-art facility, serving over 500 clients across 40+ countries. Our team of 200+ skilled engineers and technicians are dedicated to delivering exceptional results on every project.

  • ISO 9001:2015 & AS9100D certified operations
  • ITAR registered for defense manufacturing
  • FDA compliant for medical device components
  • Lean manufacturing principles across all operations
  • Continuous improvement culture with Six Sigma practices

Mission & Values

What Drives Us Forward

Our core values shape every decision, every process, and every relationship at FORXA.
