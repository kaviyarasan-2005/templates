Our Story

Building the Future of IT Support

Founded in 2010, TechAssist started with a simple mission: make enterprise-grade IT support accessible to businesses of all sizes. What began as a small team of three engineers has grown into a 200+ person organization serving over 2,500 clients worldwide.

We believe that technology should empower businesses, not hold them back. That's why we've built a proactive, client-first approach to IT support that focuses on preventing problems before they occur.
