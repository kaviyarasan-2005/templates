Help Center

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions.

Home Help Center
General
What IT support services do you offer?
We offer a full range of IT support services including network management, cloud solutions, cybersecurity, data backup and recovery, help desk support, and strategic IT consulting. Our services are customizable to fit businesses of all sizes.
What are your business hours?
Our standard business hours are Monday through Friday, 8AM to 8PM EST. However, we provide 24/7 emergency support for critical issues. Enterprise plan customers receive round-the-clock dedicated support.
How quickly do you respond to support tickets?
Our average response time is under 15 minutes for critical issues and under 2 hours for standard requests. Response times may vary by plan — Enterprise customers enjoy priority response with dedicated engineers.
Billing & Plans
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the change takes effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer a free trial?
We offer a free 14-day trial on our Pro plan so you can experience our full suite of services. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose the plan that best fits your needs.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via purchase orders and invoicing with NET-30 terms.
Technical
Do you provide on-site support?
Yes, we provide on-site support for hardware issues, network installations, and situations that can't be resolved remotely. On-site visits are included in Pro and Enterprise plans. Basic plan customers can request on-site support at an additional fee.
What platforms and operating systems do you support?
We support all major platforms including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. Our cloud solutions are compatible with AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. We also support most enterprise software and custom applications.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is ready to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
