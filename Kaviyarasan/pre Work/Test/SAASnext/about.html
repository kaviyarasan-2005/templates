ABOUT US

BUILDING THE FUTURE
OF CLOUD STORAGE

Founded in 2019, CloudVault is on a mission to make secure cloud storage accessible to everyone.

CloudVault was born from a simple observation: existing cloud storage solutions were either secure but difficult to use, or easy to use but compromised on security. We set out to build something better.

Today, we serve over 500,000 users worldwide, from individual freelancers to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our platform handles billions of file operations every month while maintaining a 99.9% uptime SLA.
