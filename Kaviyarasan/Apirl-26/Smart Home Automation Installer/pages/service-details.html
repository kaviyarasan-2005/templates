Why Upgrade to Smart Lighting?

Smart lighting goes far beyond simply controlling your lights from a phone. Our systems create dynamic, responsive environments that adapt to your daily routines — brightening gradually in the morning, dimming automatically at sunset, and securing your home with presence simulation when you're away.

Compatible with Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, and the SmartNest platform, our installations integrate seamlessly with whatever ecosystem you prefer.

What's Included

  • Free pre-installation consultation and lighting design plan
  • Professional installation of all smart bulbs, switches, and dimmers
  • Scene creation (Morning, Movie, Party, Sleep, Away, etc.)
  • Voice assistant setup and configuration
  • SmartNest app configuration and walkthrough
  • 12-month installation warranty + 5-year support plan
