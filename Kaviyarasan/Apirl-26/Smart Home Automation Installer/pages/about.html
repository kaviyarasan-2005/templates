About SmartNest

We Make Smart Living Simple

Since 2012, we've been on a mission to bring professional-grade home automation to every household — without the complexity.

Our Story

From a Garage Workshop to 1,500+ Smart Homes

SmartNest was founded in 2012 by Marcus and Elena Chen, two electrical engineers frustrated by how complicated home automation was for the average homeowner. They started in a small garage workshop in Austin, TX, hand-building custom automation panels for neighbors and friends.

Word spread quickly. What began as a weekend side project became a full-fledged business within 18 months. Today, SmartNest has a team of 48 certified technicians, a proprietary installation platform, and a client satisfaction rate of 98%.

Our philosophy has never changed: every home deserves to be smart, and every homeowner deserves an installer they can trust. We don't sell products — we deliver experiences.
