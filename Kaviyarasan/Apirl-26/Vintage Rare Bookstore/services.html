Preservation Guide

Care Tips for Your Private Collection

Small adjustments in storage and handling can significantly extend the life of your antique volumes.

Light Control

Avoid direct sunlight. UV rays deteriorate paper fibers and fade pigments. Use UV-filtering glass for display cases.

Climate Control

Maintain a steady 18-20C and 45-50% humidity. Fluctuations cause paper to expand and contract, leading to cracking.

Proper Shelving

Store books vertically, snugly but not tightly. Use bookends for support to prevent spine leaning and warping.

Careful Handling

Clean, dry hands are best. Avoid using pressure on the spine when opening, and never pull by the top of the spine.
