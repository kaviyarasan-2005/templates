FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a kitchen remodel take?
Most kitchen remodels take 4–12 weeks depending on scope. A basic refresh can be done in 2–3 weeks, while a full luxury transformation may take 10–16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline before any work begins.
Do I need to vacate my home during renovation?
For kitchen renovations, you do not need to vacate. We set up dust barriers and schedule noisy work during agreed hours. For full home renovations we recommend temporary accommodation.
What is included in your warranty?
Our 5-year workmanship warranty covers all labor and installation. Material warranties (from manufacturers) are passed on to you. We handle any warranty claim on your behalf at no cost.
Can I supply my own appliances or materials?
Absolutely. You can supply your own appliances, tiles, fixtures, or any materials. We recommend discussing this upfront to ensure compatibility and to adjust the quote accordingly.
Are permits included?
Yes. For work requiring permits (structural, electrical, plumbing), we handle all permit applications and inspections on your behalf. Permit fees are included in the project quote.
