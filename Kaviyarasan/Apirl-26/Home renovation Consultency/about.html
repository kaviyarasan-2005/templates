Our Story

15 Years of Building Dreams

Founded in 2009 by master carpenter Daniel Hayes and interior architect Sofia Reyes, Renovo began as a two-person operation driven by a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a renovation experience that is transparent, timely, and transformative.

Today, with a team of 50+ licensed craftsmen, designers, and project managers, we have completed over 2,500 projects across California — from modest bathroom updates to multi-million dollar estate renovations.

Our mission remains unchanged: deliver exceptional quality, maintain complete transparency, and create spaces that elevate the way our clients live.
