Professional Art Services

Comprehensive solutions for artists, collectors, and institutions. From meticulous restoration to visionary curation.

01

Exhibition Curation

Our expert curators combine deep art historical knowledge with contemporary spatial design to create exhibitions that tell compelling visual stories. We handle everything from artwork selection to spatial planning and narrative development.

  • Thematic narrative development
  • Spatial design & layout
  • Lighting optimization

02

Art Restoration

Preserve the beauty and value of your collection with our meticulous restoration and conservation services. Our master conservators utilize both traditional techniques and modern scientific analysis to breathe new life into aging or damaged works.

  • Oil painting conservation
  • Varnish removal & re-varnishing
  • Tear repair & relining
Learn More
03

Custom Framing

Enhance and protect your artworks with museum-quality custom framing. We source the finest materials globally, offering conservation-grade matting, UV-protective glazing, and bespoke frame profiles designed to complement each specific piece.

  • Conservation-grade materials
  • Hand-finished mouldings
  • Anti-reflective UV glass
