Our Story

A Legacy of Movement

Founded in 2010 by Sofia Martinez and Marcus Johnson, Alan began as a small 800 sq ft space with a big dream: to create a welcoming environment where anyone could discover the transformative power of dance.

What started with just three classes and a handful of students has grown into a thriving dance community with over 2,500 active members, 45 world-class instructors, and a state-of-the-art facility spanning 15,000 sq ft with 8 specialized studios.

Today, Alan is recognized as one of the premier dance studios in the country, producing award-winning performers and nurturing dancers of every age, background, and skill level.

Our Journey

Studio History

Key milestones in our dance story

2010

The Beginning

Alan opens its doors with 3 instructors and a single studio room. Our first class had just 8 students.

2013

First Expansion

Grew to 4 studios and 15 instructors. Launched our kids' dance program and hip-hop curriculum.

2016

National Recognition

Won "Best Dance Studio" at the National Dance Awards. Students performed at major events nationwide.

2019

New Headquarters

Moved to our current 15,000 sq ft facility with 8 studios, a performance theater, and recovery lounge.

2022

Digital Platform

Launched our online booking platform and virtual classes, serving students across 20+ countries.

2026

The Future

2,500+ students, 45 instructors, and expanding. Opening our second location this coming fall.
