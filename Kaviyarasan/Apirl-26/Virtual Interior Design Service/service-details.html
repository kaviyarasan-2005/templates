Residential Design Makeover

Overview

Our residential design service is built around understanding how you live. We focus on transforming your basic living quarters into a cohesive, aesthetically pleasing, and functionally optimized sanctuary.

By leveraging advanced 3D virtual modeling, you get to explore your new home before a single piece of furniture is ordered. This package covers everything from space planning and palette selection to custom millwork design and shoppable furniture lists.

Our Process

1. Discovery Call

We define your aesthetic preferences, functional needs, and project budget.

2. Concepts & Floorplans

We provide 2D layouts and mood boards for your initial review.

3. 3D Renderings

Experience your space via photorealistic 3D virtual tours.

4. Final Deliverables

Receive your exact shopping list, paint codes, and implementation guide.
