About This Class

Our Classical Ballet program is designed for dancers of all levels who want to master the elegant art of ballet. From your first plié to advanced pointe work, our expert instructors guide you through a structured curriculum that builds strength, flexibility, and artistry.

Classes follow the Royal Academy of Dance (RAD) syllabus, ensuring internationally recognized standards of excellence. Each session includes a full barre warm-up, center exercises, across-the-floor combinations, and cool-down stretching.

What You'll Learn

  • Proper ballet terminology and positions (1st through 5th)
  • Barre technique including pliés, tendus, dégagés, and grands battements
  • Center work: adagio, pirouettes, allegro, and petit allegro
  • Port de bras and upper body carriage
  • Musicality and artistic expression
  • Injury prevention and proper alignment

What You Need

  • Ballet slippers (pink for women, black for men)
  • Leotard and tights (colors specified per level)
  • Hair secured in a bun (for long hair)
  • Water bottle
  • No prior experience needed for Beginner level

Level Requirements

  • Beginner: No experience needed. Perfect for first-time dancers.
  • Intermediate: 1+ years of ballet training. Comfortable at barre.
  • Advanced: 3+ years. Strong technique, ready for pointe work.
