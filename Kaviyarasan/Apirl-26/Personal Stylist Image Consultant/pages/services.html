FAQs

Common Questions

Do I need a large budget to work with you?
Not at all. We work with a wide range of budgets and always prioritise quality over quantity. Our goal is to build a wardrobe that works within your means.
How do virtual styling sessions work?
Via video call, we conduct all the same analysis as an in-person session. You'll receive a full digital style guide, shopping links, and outfit boards delivered within 48 hours.
How many sessions will I need?
Most clients see dramatic results in 2-3 sessions. However, we offer flexible packages from a single consultation to comprehensive ongoing support.
What if I don't like the choices made for me?
We guarantee your satisfaction. If any purchased item doesn't feel right, we'll work with you until it does. Your comfort and confidence are our priority.
