Personal Shopping

Curated, handpicked selections that perfectly match your style profile, body type, and lifestyle needs.

What Is Personal Shopping?

Our Personal Shopping service is a fully bespoke experience designed to take the stress out of shopping while elevating the quality of your wardrobe. Your dedicated stylist conducts a detailed pre-session analysis of your style goals, lifestyle needs, and body proportions.

During the session, we visit carefully selected boutiques and stores — or curate an edit of online options — choosing pieces that are precisely right for you. Every item selected adheres to your colour palette, body guidelines, and style identity.
