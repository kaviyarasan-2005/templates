Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you purchase rare books from private collections?
Yes, we are always looking to acquire exceptional single items or entire libraries. Please use the contact form above and select "I Want to Sell Books" as your subject. We will ask for detailed photographs and provenance information before arranging an in-person appraisal.
How long does a typical book restoration take?
Restoration timelines vary significantly depending on the book's condition and the complexity of the required work. Minor spine repairs may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive conservation of severely damaged volumes can take up to 6 months. We will provide a detailed timeline along with our quote.
Do you offer certificates of authenticity?
Absolutely. Every book purchased from VintStore comes with a comprehensive certificate of authenticity signed by our Chief Curator, detailing the book's condition, historical significance, and any known provenance.
Can I arrange a private physical viewing at your London store?
Yes, private viewings in our secure reading room can be arranged by appointment for any item in our catalogue exceeding £5,000 in value. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance so our staff can prepare the materials.
