About This Vehicle

The Tiffin Allegro Bus 45OPP represents the ultimate expression of RV luxury. This stunning 45-foot Class A coach is built on a Spartan K3 chassis with a Cummins diesel engine, offering a smooth, powerful ride on any highway.

Step inside to find a breathtaking interior with genuine hardwood cabinetry, hand-glazed tile, and premium appliances. The residential-style kitchen features a four-burner range with oven, a full-size refrigerator, and granite countertops. The master suite boasts a king-size memory foam bed and a luxurious full bath with a spa-style shower.
