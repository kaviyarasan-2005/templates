Exhibition Curation

Flagship Service

What We Deliver

Our Exhibition Curation service is the centrepiece of everything we do at Lumière. It encompasses the full lifecycle of an exhibition — from initial conceptual development through to final deinstallation — managed by a dedicated team of curators, art historians, and logistical specialists.

"An exhibition is not a collection of objects. It is a carefully constructed argument — a physical essay about what art can do and mean."— Eleanor Whitfield, Gallery Director

We begin every project with an extended discovery phase, working closely with you to understand the thematic territory, the audience you wish to reach, and the artists whose work will carry the exhibition's ideas.

The installation phase is managed by our professional art handlers, working to museum conservation standards — ensuring every artwork is displayed in conditions that protect its integrity while maximising its visual impact.
