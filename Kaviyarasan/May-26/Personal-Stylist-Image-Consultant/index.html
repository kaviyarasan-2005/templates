Our Mission

Art That Transforms Communities

Founded in 2018, Lumière Gallery began as a single curated space in Manhattan with a radical belief: that great art should be accessible, that beauty is a public good, and that every community deserves world-class cultural infrastructure.

Today, we operate across three cities, partner with over 120 independent artists, and welcome more than 50,000 visitors annually. Our curatorial philosophy blends classical mastery with bold contemporary voices.
