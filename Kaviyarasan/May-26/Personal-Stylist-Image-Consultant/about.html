Our Story

A gallery founded on the belief that extraordinary art should be accessible to everyone.

Our Purpose

Art Is Not a
Luxury. It's a
Necessity.

We believe art has the power to shift perspectives, build empathy, and transform communities. Lumière Gallery exists to make those transformations happen — through exceptional curatorial programming, genuine artist relationships, and radical accessibility.

Our vision is a world where every person has regular access to inspiring, challenging, and beautiful art — not as a privilege of the wealthy, but as a fundamental part of a well-lived life.

Milestones

How We Got Here

Six years of bold exhibitions, daring acquisitions, and community-building moments that shaped who we are today.

2018

The First Gallery Opens

A 2,000 sq ft space in Manhattan's Chelsea district becomes home to Lumière's inaugural exhibition: "New Voices in Contemporary Abstraction" featuring eight emerging artists.

2020

National Recognition

Our "Form & Void" sculpture exhibition wins the National Arts Council Award for Outstanding Exhibition Design. Attendance surpasses 15,000 visitors over its 8-week run.

2022

Digital Gallery Launch

Responding to a new digital-first art world, we launch our virtual gallery platform, hosting 18 online exhibitions in the first year, reaching collectors in 40+ countries.

2024

Three Cities, One Vision

Lumière expands to Los Angeles and London, completing a transatlantic gallery network. We now partner with over 120 artists and welcome 50,000+ visitors annually.
