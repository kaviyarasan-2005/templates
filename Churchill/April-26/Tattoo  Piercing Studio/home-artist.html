FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing varies based on design complexity, size, placement, and time required. Small pieces start at $150, while larger custom work is typically $200/hour. We offer free consultations to provide accurate quotes.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Get a good night's sleep, eat a full meal beforehand, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.

Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Pain levels vary depending on placement and individual tolerance. Areas over bone or thin skin (ribs, ankles) tend to be more sensitive. Many describe it as a scratching or vibrating sensation. We offer numbing cream for sensitive areas.

How long does healing take?

Surface healing typically takes 2-3 weeks, while complete healing of deeper layers takes 2-3 months. Following our aftercare instructions carefully ensures the best results and fastest healing.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We welcome walk-ins for smaller pieces based on artist availability. For custom work, we recommend booking a consultation at least 2 weeks in advance to allow time for design creation.
