Our Story

From a Small Roastery to Your Favorite Cup

Since 2015, Ember & Brew has been on a mission to bring exceptional specialty coffee to every cup. Our journey began in a modest roastery with a second-hand drum roaster and an unwavering passion for quality.

Today, we partner with sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond — sourcing only the top 3% of arabica beans. Every batch is meticulously roasted in small batches to unlock each origin's unique personality.

Farm To Cup

Our Roasting Process

Every cup tells a story — from the farms we partner with to the roasting profiles we develop.

Sourcing & Selection

We travel to origin farms across Africa, Central & South America to hand-select green beans at peak harvest. Only the top 3% make the cut.

Quality Control & Cupping

Every lot undergoes rigorous cupping sessions. Our Q-graders evaluate aroma, acidity, body, and aftertaste before approving any batch.

Small-Batch Roasting

Using our Probat drum roaster, each batch is carefully roasted to a custom profile designed to highlight the bean's unique characteristics.

Freshly Packed & Delivered

Beans are packed within 24 hours of roasting in nitrogen-flushed bags to lock in freshness. Shipped directly to your door.
