🌿 Our Story

About EcoClean Pro

Since 2011, we've been on a mission to make the world cleaner — one green product at a time. Meet the team and discover why we're Portland's most trusted eco-friendly cleaning service.

Our Mission

Clean Spaces, Healthy Planet

We believe that a clean environment starts with clean practices. Every product, every process, every person — aligned with nature.

Sustainability First

Every decision we make considers its environmental footprint. From biodegradable products to zero-waste packaging.

Safety & Trust

Background-checked, insured professionals using non-toxic formulas safe for families, pets, and the planet.

Excellence

We set the bar high — 98% satisfaction rate, rigorous quality checks, and a re-clean guarantee on every job.

Community Impact

10% of profits go to local environmental nonprofits. We clean homes and give back to the communities we serve.
