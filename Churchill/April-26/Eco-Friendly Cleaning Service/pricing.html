FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each cleaning visit?
Every visit includes our core eco-friendly cleaning: kitchen, bathrooms, living areas, vacuuming, mopping, and dusting. Higher tiers add deep appliance cleaning, window washing, cabinet interiors, and more.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with no penalties. We believe in earning your business with every visit.
Are there any long-term contracts?
No contracts, ever. Our recurring plans offer the best value, but you're free to cancel at any time. We offer a 20% discount for recurring bookings.
Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes! We bring all cleaning products and equipment. Our proprietary plant-based formulas are non-toxic, biodegradable, and certified safe for children and pets.
