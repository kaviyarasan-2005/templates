Our Story

A quarter century of preserving the art, warmth, and beauty of vinyl records.

Our Mission

Keeping Vinyl Culture Alive

Vintage Vinyl was born from a simple belief: music sounds best on vinyl. Since opening our doors in Brooklyn in 2001, we've grown from a small corner shop into the city's most beloved record destination.

We curate every record by hand, ensuring each piece meets our standards for quality and sound. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just discovering the magic of analog sound, our team is here to guide your journey.

Our store is more than a retail space — it's a community hub where music lovers gather, share stories, and discover new sounds together.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🎵

Authenticity

Every record is graded and verified. We guarantee the provenance and quality of each pressing in our store.

🤝

Community

We host listening parties, album release events, and workshops to bring music lovers together.

🌍

Sustainability

We believe in the circular economy — buying, selling, and trading records to reduce waste.

🔊

Sound Quality

We're obsessed with audio fidelity. From 180g pressings to tube amplifiers, we pursue sonic perfection.

Our Journey

25 Years of Vinyl

2001

The Beginning

Robert Kelley opens a small 500 sq ft record shop on Groove Street, Brooklyn, with a personal collection of 2,000 records.

2008

Growing the Community

Launch of weekly listening parties and vinyl swap meets. Store expands to 2,000 sq ft.

2015

The Audiophile Expansion

Added listening stations with high-end audio equipment and launched our professional cleaning service.

2023

Going Online

Launched our e-commerce platform, bringing our curated collection to vinyl lovers worldwide.
