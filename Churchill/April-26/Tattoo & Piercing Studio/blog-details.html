Essential Tattoo Aftercare: The Complete Guide

MS
Marcus Steel
Mar 28, 20265 min read

Getting a new tattoo is exciting, but proper aftercare is crucial. How you care for your tattoo in the first few weeks determines how well it heals and how vibrant the colors stay over time.

The First 24 Hours

Your artist will apply a bandage or wrap after completing your tattoo. Keep it on for 2-4 hours. When ready to remove it, wash your hands thoroughly with antibacterial soap first.

"The first 48 hours are the most critical. Treat your new tattoo like an open wound — because that's exactly what it is."

— Marcus Steel, Lead Artist

Days 2-14: Active Healing

Wash your tattoo 2-3 times daily with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap. Pat dry gently with a clean paper towel — never rub. Apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare ointment.

  • Avoid submerging in water (pools, baths, hot tubs)
  • Keep out of direct sunlight
  • Don't scratch or pick at peeling skin
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing over the area
  • Avoid intense exercise that causes excessive sweating

Long-Term Care

Once fully healed (2-3 months), your tattoo still needs protection. Always apply SPF 30+ sunscreen when exposed to UV rays. Keep skin moisturized, stay hydrated, and your tattoo will maintain its vibrancy for decades.

Questions about aftercare? Don't hesitate to contact us. We offer free follow-up consultations and touch-ups within 3 months of your session.
