FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from our clients and partners.

What types of manufacturing services does FORXA offer?

FORXA offers a comprehensive range of manufacturing services including 5-axis CNC machining, metal fabrication (laser cutting, bending, welding), rapid prototyping (3D printing, CNC prototyping), electro-mechanical assembly, quality control and inspection, and supply chain management. We serve aerospace, automotive, medical, defense, energy, and industrial sectors.

What materials can you work with?

We work with a wide range of materials including aluminum alloys (6061, 7075, 2024), stainless steel (303, 304, 316, 17-4PH), carbon steel, tool steel, titanium (Grade 2, Grade 5), Inconel, Hastelloy, brass, copper, and engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, UHMW, Nylon). We also handle specialty and exotic alloys for defense and aerospace applications.

What are your typical lead times?

Standard lead times range from 5-15 business days depending on complexity, material availability, and quantity. Rush services are available with lead times as short as 48 hours for critical needs. Prototypes can often be completed in 3-5 business days. We recommend contacting us early for large production runs to secure optimal scheduling.

What quality certifications does FORXA hold?

FORXA is ISO 9001:2015 certified for quality management, AS9100D certified for aerospace quality requirements, ITAR registered for defense manufacturing, and FDA compliant for medical device components. We also maintain NADCAP accreditation for special processes and are UL certified for specific product categories.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Getting a quote is easy! Simply send your drawings (CAD files preferred — STEP, IGES, SolidWorks, or PDF) along with material specifications, quantities, and any special requirements to sales@forxa.com or use our online contact form. Our engineering team typically provides detailed quotes within 24-48 hours for standard projects.

Do you offer design for manufacturability (DFM) consultation?

Yes! Our experienced engineers provide complimentary DFM reviews with every quote. We analyze your designs for potential manufacturing challenges, suggest improvements to reduce cost and improve quality, and work collaboratively with your team to optimize parts for production. This service is included at no additional charge for Professional and Enterprise plan members.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We have no strict minimum order quantity. We handle everything from single prototype pieces to production runs of 100,000+ parts. Our flexible manufacturing setup allows us to efficiently handle any volume. For very small quantities, we recommend our Starter plan which is designed for low-volume, high-mix production needs.

How do you handle intellectual property and confidentiality?

We take IP protection very seriously. All employees sign comprehensive NDAs, and we execute mutual NDAs with every client before receiving drawings. Our facility is access-controlled, our IT systems are enterprise-grade secured, and we are ITAR-registered, meaning we meet the strictest government standards for information security. Your designs and specifications are kept strictly confidential.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us anytime and we'll get back to you promptly.
