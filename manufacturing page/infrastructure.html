Our Facility

World-Class Manufacturing Capabilities

Our facility is equipped with the latest technology to handle projects from prototype to full production across multiple industries.

CNC Machine Shop

50+ CNC machines including 5-axis milling centers, multi-axis lathes, Swiss-type automatics, and wire EDM machines running 24/7 with automated workpiece handling.

Fabrication Bay

10,000 sq. ft. fabrication area with fiber laser cutters, plasma tables, CNC press brakes, robotic welding cells, and dedicated grinding and deburring stations.

Metrology Laboratory

Climate-controlled inspection lab with Zeiss CMMs, optical comparators, surface profilometers, hardness testers, and spectroscopic material analyzers.

Clean Room Assembly

ISO Class 7 clean room for medical device and electronics assembly. ESD-safe workstations, automated torque systems, and functional test equipment.

Equipment Highlights

Cutting-Edge Machinery

50+ CNC Machines

Including DMG Mori 5-axis centers, Mazak multi-tasking machines, Citizen Swiss-type lathes, and Makino high-speed machining centers for every application.

Robotic Automation

FANUC and KUKA robotic cells for machine tending, welding, palletizing, and inspection. Automated guided vehicles for material transport across the facility.

Advanced Metrology

Zeiss Contura & PRISMO CMMs, Keyence digital microscopes, Mitutoyo roundness testers, and X-ray fluorescence analyzers for complete material verification.
