Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No hidden fees. Setup charges for new tooling are quoted upfront and amortized across your production volume. All prices include standard packaging and domestic shipping.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with prorated adjustments.

What if my project requires more than the plan allows?

Additional parts beyond your plan limit are billed at competitive per-unit rates. We'll always notify you before any overage charges apply and can discuss upgrading if it makes more financial sense.
