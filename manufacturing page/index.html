About Our Company

Building the Future with Precision & Innovation

Founded in 2001, FORXA Manufacturing has grown from a small machine shop into a full-service manufacturing powerhouse serving aerospace, automotive, medical, and industrial sectors across 40+ countries. Our 200,000 sq. ft. facility houses the latest in manufacturing technology.

  • ISO 9001:2015 & AS9100D certified for aerospace quality
  • 200+ skilled engineers and technicians on staff
  • State-of-the-art 200,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility
  • Serving 500+ clients across 40+ countries globally
  • 24/7 production capability with rapid turnaround
