What We Offer

End-to-End Manufacturing Services

From initial design consultation to final delivery, FORXA provides complete manufacturing solutions across multiple disciplines and industries.

Precision

5-Axis CNC Machining

Advanced multi-axis CNC machining for complex geometries. We handle everything from prototypes to high-volume production with tolerances as tight as ±0.001mm. Our fleet of 50+ CNC machines operates 24/7 to meet your deadlines.

Learn More
Fabrication

Metal Fabrication & Welding

Full-service metal fabrication including laser cutting, plasma cutting, press brake forming, and certified welding (TIG, MIG, Spot). We work with steel, aluminum, stainless, titanium, and specialty alloys for structural and cosmetic applications.

Learn More
Quality

Quality Control & Testing

ISO-certified quality management system with in-house CMM inspection, optical measurement, hardness testing, surface roughness analysis, and NDT capabilities. First article inspection and statistical process control on every production run.

Learn More
Assembly

Electro-Mechanical Assembly

Complete assembly services from sub-assemblies to full system integration. Wire harness fabrication, PCB assembly, mechanical assembly, functional testing, and clean room packaging for sensitive components and medical devices.

Learn More
Speed

Rapid Prototyping

Accelerate your product development with our rapid prototyping services. 3D printing (SLA, SLS, DMLS), CNC prototyping, and bridge production. From concept to first article in days with design for manufacturability consultation included.

Learn More
Logistics

Supply Chain Management

Integrated supply chain solutions including vendor-managed inventory, Kanban systems, JIT delivery, material sourcing, and vendor consolidation. Reduce your supplier base and total cost of ownership with our turnkey approach.
